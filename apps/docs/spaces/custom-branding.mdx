## Overview

Custom Branding is a Pro feature that lets you replace Rallly's default styling with your own brand identity. When enabled, your chosen primary color is applied to your public poll pages and outgoing emails.

## Requirements

- A Pro space subscription
- Space Owner or Admin role

## Enabling Custom Branding

1. Go to **Settings → General** in your space.
2. Under the **Branding** section, toggle on **Enable Custom Branding**.
3. Use the color picker to choose your primary color.
4. Click **Save** to apply the color.

Once enabled, your primary color will be used on all public-facing poll pages and emails sent to participants.

<Tip>
  Upload your logo and give your space a custom name.
</Tip>

## Disabling Custom Branding

Toggle off **Enable Custom Branding** in **Settings → General**. Rallly's default styling will be restored immediately.
